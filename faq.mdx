## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is ACE Copilot?" icon="circle-question">
    ACE Copilot is a Windows desktop application designed for corporate visual metrics and traceability management. It enables organizations to:
    
    - Track and visualize key performance indicators (KPIs)
    - Manage traceability matrices for compliance and project management
    - Create interactive dashboards for data-driven decision making
    - Generate professional reports and exports
    - Collaborate with team members on shared metrics
    
    ACE Copilot is particularly valuable for project managers, quality assurance teams, compliance officers, and business analysts who need to track, visualize, and report on corporate metrics.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's new in version 2.3?" icon="star">
    Version 2.3 introduces several significant enhancements:
    
    - **Enhanced Visual Corporate Metrics:** Redesigned dashboard components with improved visualization capabilities
    - **Advanced Traceability Management:** Bi-directional linking and matrix views
    - **Custom Metric Templates:** Save and reuse metric configurations
    - **Real-time Collaboration:** Share live dashboards with team members
    - **New Export Formats:** PowerPoint and interactive HTML reports
    - **Performance Improvements:** 40% faster rendering, 25% less memory usage
    
    See the [Releases](/releases) page for complete changelog.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is ACE Copilot free to use?" icon="dollar-sign">
    ACE Copilot offers several licensing options:
    
    - **Community Edition:** Free for individual use with basic features
    - **Professional:** Full-featured license for individual users
    - **Team:** Multi-user licenses with collaboration features
    - **Enterprise:** Organization-wide licensing with priority support
    
    The application can be downloaded and evaluated free for 30 days with full Professional features. After the trial period, you can continue with Community Edition features or purchase a license.
    
    <Note>
      Academic and non-profit organizations may qualify for discounted licensing. Contact your license administrator for details.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use ACE Copilot on multiple computers?" icon="computer">
    License terms depend on your license type:
    
    - **Community Edition:** Can be used on any number of computers
    - **Professional:** Single-user license allows installation on up to 2 computers for the same user
    - **Team/Enterprise:** Named or concurrent user licensing depending on agreement
    
    Your license key can be deactivated on one machine and activated on another through Settings > License > Manage Activations.
    
    You cannot run ACE Copilot simultaneously on multiple machines with a single Professional license.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does ACE Copilot work on Mac or Linux?" icon="apple">
    Currently, ACE Copilot is available only for Windows (Windows 10 or later). 
    
    However, you can run ACE Copilot on Mac or Linux using:
    - **Parallels Desktop** (Mac)
    - **VMware Fusion** (Mac)
    - **Wine** (Linux - experimental, not officially supported)
    - **Windows virtual machine** on any platform
    
    <Warning>
      Running ACE Copilot in virtual machines or compatibility layers is not officially supported and may result in performance issues or reduced functionality.
    </Warning>
    
    Native Mac and Linux versions are under consideration for future releases based on user demand.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where is my data stored?" icon="folder">
    ACE Copilot stores all data locally on your computer by default:
    
    - **Project data:** `%AppData%\AceCopilot\Data`
    - **Settings:** `%AppData%\AceCopilot\settings.json`
    - **Backups:** `%AppData%\AceCopilot\Backups`
    
    This typically translates to: `C:\Users\[YourUsername]\AppData\Roaming\AceCopilot\`
    
    **Optional cloud storage:** If you enable cloud backup (Professional/Team/Enterprise only), encrypted backups are stored in your configured cloud storage provider (OneDrive, Google Drive, or Dropbox).
    
    You maintain full control over your data and can export projects at any time using File > Export Project.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## System Requirements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the minimum system requirements?" icon="desktop">
    **Minimum Requirements:**
    - **Operating System:** Windows 10 (version 1809) or later
    - **Processor:** Intel Core i3 or equivalent (2 GHz+)
    - **RAM:** 4 GB
    - **Storage:** 500 MB available space
    - **Display:** 1366 x 768 resolution
    - **.NET Framework:** 4.8 or higher
    - **Graphics:** DirectX 11 compatible graphics card
    
    **Recommended for Optimal Performance:**
    - **Operating System:** Windows 11
    - **Processor:** Intel Core i5 or equivalent (3 GHz+)
    - **RAM:** 8 GB or more
    - **Storage:** 2 GB available space (SSD preferred)
    - **Display:** 1920 x 1080 or higher resolution
    - **Graphics:** Dedicated GPU with 2GB VRAM
    
    <Tip>
      For large datasets (>10,000 records) or complex dashboards with multiple real-time visualizations, 16GB RAM and an SSD are highly recommended.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much disk space does ACE Copilot use?" icon="hard-drive">
    - **Application installation:** ~300 MB
    - **Required free space:** 500 MB (includes temporary files and cache)
    - **Data storage:** Varies based on your projects
      - Small projects (1,000 records): ~10 MB
      - Medium projects (10,000 records): ~50 MB
      - Large projects (100,000 records): ~300 MB
    
    Additional space is required for:
    - Automatic backups (configurable retention)
    - Export files (PDF, Excel, PowerPoint)
    - Cache and temporary files
    
    You can configure cache size and backup retention in Settings > Performance and Settings > Backups respectively.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does ACE Copilot require an internet connection?" icon="wifi">
    **No**, ACE Copilot works fully offline once installed. Internet connectivity is only required for:
    
    - Initial license activation
    - Checking for software updates
    - Cloud backup/sync features (optional)
    - API integrations with external services (optional)
    - Sending scheduled email reports (optional)
    - Real-time collaboration features (Team/Enterprise)
    
    All core functionality—creating dashboards, importing data, generating reports, and analyzing metrics—works completely offline.
    
    <Note>
      If you're behind a corporate firewall or proxy, you may need to configure proxy settings in Settings > Network for online features to work.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file formats does ACE Copilot support?" icon="file">
    **Import Formats:**
    - CSV (UTF-8, UTF-16, ASCII)
    - Excel (.xlsx, .xlsm)
    - JSON
    - XML
    - SQL database connections (PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server)
    - REST API endpoints
    
    **Export Formats:**
    - PDF (dashboards and reports)
    - Excel (.xlsx)
    - PowerPoint (.pptx) - new in v2.3
    - CSV
    - JSON
    - Interactive HTML
    - PNG, JPG (chart exports)
    
    **Project Files:**
    - .aceproject (native ACE Copilot project format)
    - .zip (archived projects with data)
    
    Maximum file size for imports: 100 MB (can be increased in Settings > Import for Professional/Enterprise licenses).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Data Security and Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my data secure in ACE Copilot?" icon="lock">
    Yes. ACE Copilot implements multiple security measures:
    
    - **Local storage:** All data stored on your computer by default, not on external servers
    - **Encryption at rest:** Project files encrypted using AES-256 (Professional/Enterprise)
    - **Encrypted backups:** Cloud backups are encrypted before upload
    - **No telemetry:** ACE Copilot does not collect or transmit usage data or project information
    - **Secure connections:** All API integrations use HTTPS/TLS
    - **Access control:** Role-based permissions for Team/Enterprise (RBAC)
    - **Audit trail:** All changes logged for compliance (Enterprise)
    
    <Warning>
      While ACE Copilot provides strong security features, you are responsible for protecting your computer with passwords, encryption, and physical security.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does ACE Copilot collect any personal information?" icon="user-secret">
    **No.** ACE Copilot respects your privacy:
    
    - No analytics or telemetry data collected
    - No personal information transmitted to developers
    - No tracking of usage patterns or behavior
    - License activation only transmits license key and machine ID (anonymized hardware hash)
    
    **Optional data transmission** only occurs when you explicitly:
    - Check for updates (transmits version number)
    - Report bugs via Help > Report Issue (only information you provide)
    - Enable cloud backup (only to your configured cloud provider)
    - Use API integrations (only to services you configure)
    
    You can disable all network communication in Settings > Privacy > Offline Mode.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use ACE Copilot with sensitive corporate data?" icon="shield-halved">
    **Yes.** ACE Copilot is designed for corporate use with sensitive data:
    
    - Fully offline operation supported
    - Data never leaves your infrastructure
    - Encryption at rest and in transit
    - Compliance features for regulated industries
    - Audit trail for data access (Enterprise)
    - No cloud dependencies for core functionality
    
    Many organizations in regulated industries (healthcare, finance, government) use ACE Copilot for compliance and quality metrics.
    
    **Best practices for sensitive data:**
    - Enable disk encryption (Windows BitLocker)
    - Use Enterprise edition with encryption at rest
    - Disable cloud backup or use organization-approved storage
    - Configure network policies to restrict API access
    - Enable audit logging
    - Train users on data handling policies
    
    <Tip>
      Enterprise customers can request security documentation including SOC 2 compliance information through their license administrator.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if my license expires?" icon="calendar-xmark">
    **Your data remains accessible**, but with limitations:
    
    - **Community Edition features:** Continue to work indefinitely
    - **Professional features:** Become view-only (can view but not edit dashboards)
    - **Your project files:** Remain on your computer and can be exported
    - **No data loss:** All your data is preserved
    
    You can:
    - View existing dashboards and reports
    - Export data to CSV, Excel, or PDF
    - Renew license to restore full functionality
    
    **Note:** Scheduled reports, API integrations, and cloud sync stop working after license expiration but can be re-enabled upon renewal.
    
    <Note>
      Grace period: You have 30 days after license expiration to renew without losing any collaboration or cloud data.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Integration and Compatibility

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can ACE Copilot integrate with other tools?" icon="puzzle-piece">
    **Yes.** ACE Copilot offers several integration options:
    
    **Direct Integrations (Professional/Enterprise):**
    - Microsoft Excel (import/export)
    - SQL databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server, Oracle)
    - REST APIs (custom endpoints)
    - JIRA (issue tracking integration)
    - Azure DevOps
    - ServiceNow
    
    **File-based Integration:**
    - Import CSV/Excel from any source
    - Export to standard formats for use in other tools
    - Scheduled exports to shared folders
    
    **API Access (Enterprise):**
    - REST API for programmatic access to ACE Copilot data
    - Webhook support for real-time notifications
    - PowerShell cmdlets for automation
    
    ACE Copilot supports connecting to various corporate data sources. See the [User Guide](/guide/overview) for detailed setup instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I automate ACE Copilot tasks?" icon="robot">
    **Yes**, several automation options are available:
    
    **Built-in Automation:**
    - Scheduled reports (daily, weekly, monthly)
    - Automatic data refresh from SQL/API sources
    - Scheduled exports to file shares
    - Email delivery of reports
    
    **Command-line Interface (Professional/Enterprise):**
    ```bash
    AceCopilot.exe --project "MyProject.aceproject" --export-pdf "report.pdf"
    ```
    
    **PowerShell Integration (Enterprise):**
    ```powershell
    Import-Module ACECopilot
    Open-ACEProject -Path "C:\Projects\Metrics.aceproject"
    Export-ACEDashboard -Name "KPIs" -Format PDF -Output "C:\Reports"
    ```
    
    **Windows Task Scheduler:**
    - Schedule ACE Copilot tasks to run unattended
    - Generate reports overnight
    - Automate data synchronization
    
    See the [Data Export](/features/data-export) guide for detailed examples on automating exports.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does ACE Copilot work with Excel macros or Power BI?" icon="chart-column">
    **Excel:**
    - ACE Copilot can import Excel files including those with macros
    - Macros are not executed during import (data only)
    - Export to Excel creates macro-free .xlsx files
    - You can run Excel macros on exported ACE Copilot data
    
    **Power BI:**
    - Not directly integrated, but you can:
      - Export ACE Copilot data to Excel/CSV and import into Power BI
      - Connect Power BI to the same SQL databases as ACE Copilot
      - Use ACE Copilot REST API (Enterprise) as Power BI data source
    
    **Complementary use:**
    Many organizations use ACE Copilot for detailed traceability and operational metrics, while using Power BI for executive dashboards and organization-wide reporting.
    
    <Tip>
      For regular Power BI integration, set up scheduled exports from ACE Copilot to a shared folder, then configure Power BI to import from that folder.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can multiple users work on the same project?" icon="users">
    **Yes**, with Team or Enterprise licenses:
    
    **Real-time Collaboration (v2.3+):**
    - Multiple users can view the same dashboard simultaneously
    - Live updates as data changes
    - User presence indicators
    - Role-based access control
    
    **Project Sharing Methods:**
    
    1. **Cloud Sync (Team/Enterprise):**
       - Project stored in OneDrive, SharePoint, or Google Drive
       - Automatic conflict resolution
       - Version history
    
    2. **Network Share:**
       - Place .aceproject file on network drive
       - Users open from shared location
       - File locking prevents conflicts
    
    3. **Centralized Server (Enterprise):**
       - ACE Copilot Server hosts projects
       - Web-based dashboard viewing
       - Multi-user concurrent editing
    
    **Permissions (Team/Enterprise):**
    - View only
    - Edit dashboards
    - Manage data
    - Administrator
    
    <Warning>
      Community and Professional editions do not support concurrent multi-user access. Projects must be shared sequentially or upgraded to Team edition.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="How do license renewals work?" icon="rotate">
    **Subscription Licensing:**
    
    - Licenses are annual subscriptions
    - Auto-renewal enabled by default (can be disabled)
    - Renewal reminder emails sent 30, 14, and 7 days before expiration
    - Same price for renewals (unless pricing changes)
    
    **Renewal Process:**
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Renewal Notification">
        Receive email reminder before expiration
      </Step>
      <Step title="Automatic Renewal">
        If auto-renewal enabled, charged automatically to payment method on file
      </Step>
      <Step title="Manual Renewal">
        If auto-renewal disabled, use renewal link in email to renew manually
      </Step>
      <Step title="License Updated">
        ACE Copilot automatically detects renewal (no re-entry needed)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    **Grace Period:**
    - 14-day grace period after expiration
    - Full features continue to work
    - Renewal without loss of service
    
    **Managing Renewals:**
    - Settings > License > Manage Subscription
    - Enable/disable auto-renewal
    - Update payment method
    - View renewal date
    
    <Warning>
      After grace period expires, Professional features become view-only. Renew promptly to avoid disruption.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade my license?" icon="arrow-up-arrow-down">

    - Immediate upgrade available
    - Pay pro-rated difference for current period
    - All features activated immediately
    - Settings > License > Upgrade
    
    **Downgrading:**
    - Takes effect at next renewal date
