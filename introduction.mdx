# Introduction to ACE Copilot

ACE Copilot is a powerful Windows desktop application designed to help organizations manage and visualize corporate metrics with comprehensive traceability tracking.

<Note>
  This documentation describes ACE Copilot version 2.3. User interface elements and menu paths may vary between versions. For the most current information, please refer to the in-application help system.
</Note>

## What is ACE Copilot?

ACE Copilot provides corporate teams with an intuitive platform to monitor, analyze, and track key performance indicators and business metrics. Built specifically for Windows environments, it combines visual dashboards with robust traceability features to give you complete visibility into your organizational data.

With version 2.3, ACE Copilot has been enhanced with advanced visual corporate metrics and comprehensive traceability capabilities, making it easier than ever to maintain oversight of your critical business processes.

## Who Should Use ACE Copilot?

ACE Copilot is ideal for:

- **Corporate Teams** managing cross-functional metrics and KPIs
- **Project Managers** tracking project performance and deliverables
- **Data Analysts** visualizing and analyzing organizational data
- **Business Leaders** monitoring strategic objectives and outcomes
- **Compliance Officers** maintaining audit trails and regulatory documentation

<Note>
  ACE Copilot is currently available exclusively for Windows operating systems. Ensure your system meets the requirements before installation.
</Note>

## System Requirements

To run ACE Copilot effectively, your system should meet the following requirements:

- **Operating System**: Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
- **Processor**: Intel Core i3 or equivalent
- **Memory**: 4 GB RAM minimum (8 GB recommended)
- **Storage**: 100 MB available disk space
- **Display**: 1280x720 minimum resolution
- **Network**: Internet connection for updates and synchronization
